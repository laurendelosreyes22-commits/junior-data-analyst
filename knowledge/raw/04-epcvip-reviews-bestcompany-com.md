## The Good

- **Variety of Services**
- **Industries Served**

### Variety of Services

EPCVIP boasts that its employees have a profound understanding of Pay Per Click (PPC), search engine, display, and email marketing.

### Industries Served

According to the company LinkedIn page, EPCVIP's services are best equipped for those in the following industries:

- Debt consolidation
- Personal loans
- Installment loans
- Company loans
- Credit monitoring
- Auto finance
- Auto insurance
- Health and beauty
- Merchant processing

## The Bad

- **Lots of Undisclosed Information**

### Lots of Undisclosed Information

While EPCVIP boasts being a superior option and employing knowledgeable employees, there is little evidence to back up these claims.

Instead of just claiming that employees have a "profound understanding," it would be better if EPCVIP included what training employees have undergone or what certifications its employees hold.

EPCVIP also claims that its "proprietary technology enables us to outperform our competition." This is a bold claim and we would like to at least see a portfolio of EPCVIP's work.

Additionally, there isn't any information on plans, contract lengths, or pricing. While it is common not to display pricing information, many companies will display tiered plans and contract duration.

gavel


## The Bottom Line

Unfortunately, there is just not enough information to recommend EPCVIP's services. The company claims to be a superior choice for businesses in the financial services industry, but there is just not enough information on the company website to back this up.

If you're looking to hire a digital marketing agency, then we suggest comparing EPCVIP to one of our top-ranked companies to ensure you're getting the best service.
