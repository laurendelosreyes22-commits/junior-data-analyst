# EPCVIP Overview

## What EPCVIP Does

EPCVIP (Golden Entropy Marketing, Inc dba EPCVIP) is a California-based financial services lead generation company founded in 2014 and headquartered in Calabasas, CA (26610 Agoura Rd, Suite 209). The company's core business is connecting consumers who are seeking financial products — personal loans, payday loans, installment loans, debt consolidation, credit monitoring, auto finance, and business loans — with the lenders and advertisers willing to fund them. EPCVIP sits in the middle of that transaction, generating tens of thousands of leads per day across PPC, search engine, display, and email marketing channels.

The company's name derives directly from its key performance metric: **EPC** stands for Earnings Per Click. According to EPCVIP's own positioning (`01-epcvip-financial-service-lead-provider-and-solutio.md`), the company claims "highest EPCs in the industry" — meaning publishers who route traffic through EPCVIP earn more per click than through competing networks.

## Business Model

EPCVIP operates a two-sided marketplace connecting two types of partners:

- **Publishers** — websites, affiliates, email marketers, and traffic sources that drive consumer traffic. Publishers register on EPCVIP's platform, send consumer loan inquiries (leads), and are paid a per-lead payout based on Insertion Order (IO) terms.
- **Advertisers / Lenders** — the financial institutions and lending companies that purchase those leads and attempt to convert them into funded loans.

The legal framework for this relationship is detailed in EPCVIP's Master Marketing Agreement (`02-register-epcvip.md`), which defines key concepts including Valid Leads, Invalid Leads, Payouts, and the compliance standards both sides must meet. The agreement requires publishers to maintain an 80% redirect rate, obtain FCRA consent from consumers, and comply with applicable laws including TCPA, CAN-SPAM, CCPA, and OLA (Online Lenders Alliance) best practices.

EPCVIP retains the right to "re-market to the Lead and Lead-related data without further obligation to Publisher" — meaning once a lead is acquired, EPCVIP owns the data and can sell or re-use it across its lender network.

## Products and Target Industries

According to EPCVIP's LinkedIn profile and BestCompany.com review (`04-epcvip-reviews-bestcompany-com.md`), the company serves the following verticals:

- Debt consolidation
- Personal loans
- Installment loans
- Company/business loans
- Credit monitoring
- Auto finance
- Auto insurance
- Health and beauty
- Merchant processing

The primary focus is unsecured consumer lending — personal and payday loans — where EPCVIP's optimization platform is most differentiated. The company targets borrowers across all credit tiers, with particular reach into near-prime and subprime consumers who are actively searching for loan options online.

## Key Differentiators

EPCVIP's CEO and founder Valeriy Zeltser — who holds an economics and mathematics background and trained as a financial analyst before becoming a self-taught programmer — built the company around a proprietary machine learning platform (`04-epcvip-top-featured-vendors-2022-financial-service.md`). Several differentiators stand out:

**1. Proprietary optimization platform.** Unlike competitors that use static landing pages, EPCVIP's platform enables A/B testing across multiple landing page variations per lender. Version 1.0 of the platform produced a 25% increase in conversion rate for each client. The platform incorporates real-time analytics, tracks gross revenue, conversion rates, and page visitors, and uses machine learning to predict sales growth per user and per segment.

**2. Landing page personalization at scale.** Zeltser identified early that "only one landing page for each lender would never work" — different pages appeal differently to different consumers. The system allows clients to test logos, headlines, images, copy, and lead forms dynamically, then automatically ties different metrics and remarketing channels together.

**3. Data depth and CFA-trained staff.** According to the Financial Services Review profile, EPCVIP employs over 50 experts including team members with CFA (Chartered Financial Analyst) degrees who understand that "data presentation is as important as the data itself." The advisory approach — gathering client information end-to-end, identifying bottlenecks, and customizing the platform — differentiates EPCVIP from vendors who simply sell clicks.

**4. Proven ROI results.** Zeltser cited a case where an appliance financing client went from $12,000/month in profit to over $5 million/year in sales within three months after EPCVIP rebuilt their software architecture, APIs, and landing pages.

## Industry Context

EPCVIP operates in a crowded ecosystem of financial lead generation companies. The AnswersEtc industry directory (`03-lead-generation-answers-etc-online-loan-software.md`) lists competitors including PartnerWeekly (founded 1997, one of the largest), Zero Parallel (claimed "largest volume lead provider"), PingLogix, Leap Theory, LeadsMarket, IT Media, Lead Economy, Round Sky, and DOT818. Within this environment, EPCVIP's technological differentiation — particularly its machine learning-driven landing page optimization — is the primary competitive claim.
